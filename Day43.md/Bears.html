Bears

Bears are mammals.
Their size range from small to big.
Their life begins when they are born.

To bear or not to bear

Bears mostly live in forests and do not tend to wander into the city.
If you want to become a household bear, then you need a license from the Department of Domestic Bear Residence. You will not be allowed a license if you have mauled a person in the last two months.
